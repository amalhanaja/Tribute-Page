A Brief of Events in Bill Gates Life :

  • 1955 – He was born on October 28th in Seatle, Washington, U.S.A. Son to William Henry Gates II and Mary Gates
  • 1967 – He gets Enrolled in the Lakeside School
  • 1973 – Gates enters Harvard University as pre-law major, but soon shift focus. He quickly runs through the university's most rigorous mathematics and graduate level computer science course
  • 1975 – From his dorm room, Gates calls MITS, the maker of the world's first personal computer. He offers to develop software for the MITS Altair. MITS eventually accept and buy his language for USD 3.000 plus Royalties. Gates takes his first leave of absence from school and start working on the software venture he refers to as Micro-Soft
  • 1976 – Gates and Allen register the trademark "Microsoft" and start work together
  • 1977 – Gates takes a second leave of absence and sets up his Microsoft software architechery job in Albuquerque,N.M., where MITS is headquartered
  • 1978 – Microsoft's year-end sales exceed 1 Million USD. Gates gains reputation for being tough, but focused.
  • 1979 – MITS Microsoft headquarters are relocated to Bellevue, Wash., a suburb of Gates’ hometown, Seattle.
  • 1981 – Microsoft incorporates and buys the rights to the operating system “DOS” from Seattle Computer Products. The system is modified and renamed MS-DOS, and the company licenses it to IBM for the company’s new personal computer.
  • 1983 – Paul Allen leaves Microsoft after developing a disease Microsoft announces Windows as an extension of its MS-DOS operating system.
  • 1986 – Microsoft headquarters move again, this time to nearby Redmond, Wash. Shortly after, the company goes public.
  • 1987 – At age 31, Gates becomes the youngest billionaire ever. He meets his future wife, Melinda French, at a Microsoft event in New York.
  • 1989 – Gates founds Corbis, an company of art and photography from public and private collections. Corbis later becomes a large art collection company.
  • 1990 – The Federal Trade Commission begins an investigation into possible collusion between IBM and Microsoft.
  • 1994 – Bill Gates gets married to Melinda who is from france the couple now share the job and Bill Gates now also works for the Bill and Melinda Gates foundation.
  • 1995 – In July, at age 39 and with a fortune of $12.9 billion, Gates becomes the world’s richest man. Later that summer, Microsoft introduces Internet Explorer to the world, as part of Windows 95.
  • 1996 – Bill Gates is interviewed by Charlie Rose where he discusses the future of the Internet.
  • 1998 – Bill Gates flew all the way to Belgium, on his way to meet business and government leaders about his job.
  • 1999 – Gates publishes another best-selling book, Business @ the Speed of Thought, about the interconnectedness of business and technology.
  • 2000 – Gates steps down as CEO of Microsoft. Gates’ former Harvard dorm-mate and right-hand man Steve Ballmer takes over the helm, while Gates becomes chief software architect.
  • 2001 – An agreement is reached between the DOJ and Microsoft on November. MGM releases the movie Antitrust, starring Tim Robbins, about a company and character loosely based on Microsoft and Bill Gates.
  • 2004 – Bill Gates is fined £800,000 for violating "premerger reporting requirements." after he purchased stock in a drug company and a waste-hauling firm. Bill Gates is appointed to the board of Berkshire Hathaway
  • 2005 – Queen Elizabeth II bestows an honorary knighthood on Bill Gates for his contribution to the united kingdom
  • 2006 – Gates announces that his role as an executive at Microsoft will be phased out over the following two years.
  • 2007 – Gates “graduates” from Harvard; the university awards him with an honorary degree. Gates gives the commencement speech, encouraging the graduates to strive for social change.
  • 2008 – Bill Gates drops out of the number 1 spot for being the richest man in the world
  • 2010 – Bill Gates gives a speech for TED talks where he discusses his vision for the future of world energy
  • 2014 – Bill Gates steps down as chairman of Microsoft to take a new role as Chairman of the Board of Founder and Technology Adviser.
  • 2015 – Bill Gates does a Reddit AMA in where he answers questions from the Reddit community.
  • 2016 – Gates is awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom, the highest civilian award of the United States. The award is given at a ceremony at the White House in Washington DC by US President Barack Obama.
